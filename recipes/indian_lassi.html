Chilled Indian Lassi

What is a Lassi?

Lassi is an Indian yogurt-based beverage with a smoothie-like consistency. It originated in the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent and is made by blending yogurt, water, and spices. There are various variations of lassi, including sweet, salty, spicy, and savory. Sweet lassi is a popular version made with yogurt, water or milk, sugar, and flavoring ingredients like cardamom, saffron, or rose water. It is a cooling, refreshing, probiotic drink perfect for the warm summer months

How To Prepare

Ingredients

  • ice cubes
  • 1 ¾ cups plain yogurt
  • 1 ½ cups ice water
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve.
